## About Panenta Organika

Panenta Organika is an agribusiness supply and sourcing partner focused on building a stable and sustainable commodity supply chain between farmers and industries.

Established in 2021, Panenta began as an initiative by agricultural and sustainability enthusiasts who saw the need for better market access, fair pricing, and structured procurement for agricultural commodities.

Today, Panenta Organika supports FMCG companies, HoReCa businesses, distributors, and manufacturers by ensuring consistent supply, clear coordination, and reliable sourcing.

We work closely with farmers, suppliers, and partners across regions to ensure quality, transparency, and long-term collaboration in every supply chain we manage.


## Our Focus

- Stable agricultural commodity supply  
- Transparent sourcing and procurement flow  
- Farmer empowerment and market access  
- Long-term B2B supply partnerships  


## Vision

To build a reliable and sustainable agricultural supply ecosystem connecting farmers and industries.


## Mission

- Ensure stable and transparent commodity supply  
- Empower farmers through fair market access  
- Provide structured sourcing and procurement coordination  
- Support industries with consistent raw material supply
